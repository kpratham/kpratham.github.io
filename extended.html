Week 50: The Snowy Peaks
POSTED FEBRUARY 25, 2014 | BY ADMIN

We’ve been to a lot of cold places on this trip – but this time we were at our coldest. The high altitude made everything a lot more work than we’d imagined. Thankfully our guides were really good and helped us through the worst of it. Reaching the snowy peak was one of the highlights of the trip – and a great way to end this amazing adventure.
